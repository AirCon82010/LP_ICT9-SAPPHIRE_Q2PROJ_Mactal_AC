What is Ninong AC Karinderya?

Discover us and let us share who we are!

Welcome to Ninong AC Karinderya, your go-to spot for authentic Filipino comfort food. We take pride in serving dishes that remind you of home—flavors passed down through generations, cooked with love, and seasoned with tradition.

At Ninong AC Karinderya, we believe in bringing people together over a hearty meal, celebrating the rich and vibrant culinary heritage of the Philippines. Whether you're here for a quick bite, a family gathering, or simply craving your favorite Pinoy classics, we’re here to serve you with warm smiles and even warmer plates.

Discover the taste of home at Ninong AC Karinderya, where every meal feels like it’s made by family, for family.
